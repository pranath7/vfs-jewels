Our Story

From Sowcarpet to Your Doorstep

VFS Jewels was born in the heart of Sowcarpet, Chennai's oldest and most trusted jewellery district — where craftsmen have shaped gold and gems for centuries.

Founded by a family with deep roots in the jewellery trade, VFS started as a small wholesale supplier serving local boutiques and resellers. Over the years, driven by customer love and a passion for accessible luxury, we grew into one of Chennai's most trusted names in premium imitation jewellery.

Every piece we craft carries our promise: the brilliance of real jewellery, at a price that doesn't compromise your budget.

Wholesale T&C 📜

Please read and accept the terms to unlock wholesale prices.

1. Wholesale Eligibility
Wholesale accounts are strictly for resellers, shop owners, and boutique operators buying for resale purposes only. Personal-use orders under wholesale pricing are prohibited.

2. Advance Payment System
To unlock wholesale prices and place orders, buyers must pay an advance:
  • First order: ₹1,000 advance payment.
  • Subsequent orders: ₹500 advance payment per order.
This advance amount is automatically adjusted (deducted) in the final invoice/bill generated by admin.

3. Damaged or Missing Claims Policy
An uninterrupted **unboxing video** starting from showing the sealed package address label to opening and inspecting every item is strictly required for damaged or missing product claims. Claims without a complete unboxing video will not be approved.

4. Returns & Exchange
We do not offer returns/exchanges unless the product is defective or damaged in transit, verified via unboxing video.
